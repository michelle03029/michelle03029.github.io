CMU Safety Escort Redesign

Client: CMU Escort Bus Users

Research Collaborators: Thomas Lappas, Alec Frank, Eric Zhou

Designer: Michelle Chen

September - December, 2018

Currently, there is only a bus application for the CMU Shuttle and no separate application for the CMU Escort Service. Therefore, the primary focus of the design of CMU Safety Escort is to help commuters easily find estimated arrival times and capacity of the bus.
