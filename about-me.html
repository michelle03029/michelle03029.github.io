Nice to Meet you!☺

I'm Michelle. I'm currently in my second year of undergrad study towards a BS at Carnegie Mellon University, with a major in Business Administration through the Tepper School of Business and a concentration in Product Design.

From a young age, I have been passionate about all forms of art from painting to photography. Since entering college, I have been involved in many different projects such as game development as well as website and phone interface design. As a designer, I work to create products tailored to the user. My work in user experience and my business skills have given me a better understanding of what the consumer wants and how to effectively communicate with diverse groups of people.

At school, I have been involved in many different clubs from Strong Women Strong Girls to Game Creation Society. In my free time, I love to travel, paint , meet new people, and explore food hotspots.
