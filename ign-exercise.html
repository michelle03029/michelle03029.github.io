Design Challenges #1: User Interface Exercise

Find any page on IGN that you believe needs a thorough redesign. Create a new mock (as low fidelity as sketches or wireframes, or as high fidelity as visual design mocks) that shows what you have changed on this page and why.

Design Challenges #2: Mobile App Exercise (Whiteboard Challenge)

IGN wants to create an app for gamers that helps them discover video games they haven’t played before. Take 30 minutes to sketch out how you would go about designing this app, anything from product goals, to user stories, to feature brainstorming, to a few user flow mocks.
